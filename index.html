Jean-David Collard
FOUNDER / PhD AI / HEAD OF PRODUCT

I am a PhD in Artificial Intelligence, with a thesis titled “Artificial Intelligence for Urban Mobility: Agent-Based Modeling, Reinforcement Learning and Game Theory”. I hold an Engineering Diploma from Arts et Métiers ParisTech—a prestigious French Grande École in engineering, often compared to Ivy League universities in the United States—and a Master’s degree in Advanced Materials and Processes for Energy and Nuclear Applications. My work explores how intelligent systems can reshape real-world challenges through collaboration, learning, and optimization.

As CPO and Head of Research, I specialize in applying Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI to create impactful, scalable software solutions. I co-founded AngelsBayTech, a development and research studio trusted by startups, scaleups, and industry leaders in Europe and the United States. We help visionaries transform complex ideas into high-performance digital products.

I also co-created ReadyPark, a collaborative parking and electric vehicle charging platform powered by satellite data, agent-based simulation, and AI-driven predictive models. This project received national and European recognition, including awards from the European Space Agency.

Beyond industry, I contribute to academia as a university lecturer in software engineering and information systems, and as a published researcher in leading international journals and conferences.

I am also the co-founder of TitanOne, a company providing AI solutions for the insurance, legal, and finance industries to protect sensitive information (IP & PII) while leveraging the full potential of LLMs and AI. At TitanOne, I lead Product Management, AI Research, and Model Fine-tuning.
